Project Overview :
Seeker of Sand is mix between a Vampire Survivor like and a hack'n slash. The player embody the champion of the desert and use it knowledge from alchemy to erease the corruption from the desert. For the demo, the goal is to survive during 3 nights in the desert. For fighting monsters, players will need to choose spell to fight and the order in which they will be launch to create fun combos.

We realize this project on the game engine Unity 2021. We wanted to have precise and more realistic lighting in our game. So, we decided to use the HDR Renderer Pipeline to have this result. This project has multiple technical challenges to face. The optimisation is one of them because we wanted to have an immense level to recreate a desert sensation and we need to have lots of enemies to show the power of the avatar.
