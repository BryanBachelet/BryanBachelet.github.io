Project Overview :
La Ville Rose is a turn-based RPG on PC. The player goal is to stop the Blue faction to repaint all the town in blue. The player will embody a truck in the exploration phase that is able to repaint the town in pink. In the combat phase, the player will have a squad soldier and the player have to defeat all the enemy.

For this project, we worked on Unreal 4 mainly in C++. My main focus task on this project was the optimization of paint on the exploration phase to allow the player to paint all it environnement without FPS lost. It was a great experience on render subject in bigger team than before.
