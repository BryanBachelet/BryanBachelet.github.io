Project Overview :
Soul Swapper is an Action platform in coop. Our goal is to reach the end of the level with help of your game partner. Each player has a role and a color assigned. The first role is the runner and the second is the shooter. They will have to swap roles because they can only interact with elements that have matching color with them.

For this project, we worked on Unreal 4 mainly in C++ and the special thing about this project was that we had the chance to use a dev kit of Switch. We used it to debug and build our game on switch. It was a good first experience with a dev kit. On this project, I work on the level elements, enemies and the porting on Switch.
